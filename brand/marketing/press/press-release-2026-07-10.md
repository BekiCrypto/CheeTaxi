# FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

**CheeTaxi Launches in Addis Ababa — Africa's Most Modern Mobility Platform**

*Passengers ride free. Drivers keep 100% of every fare — pay one subscription, drive unlimited.*

**ADDIS ABABA, ETHIOPIA — July 10, 2026** — CheeTaxi Technologies today announced the launch of CheeTaxi, the most modern mobility platform designed for Africa. Starting today, residents of Addis Ababa can download the CheeTaxi app and request rides across 12 transport modes — from taxis and motorcycles to parcel delivery and airport transfers.

CheeTaxi differentiates itself from existing ride-hailing platforms through a radically driver-friendly business model. Passengers ride free of platform charges — paying only the trip fare. Drivers pay one flat subscription (starting at Br 100 per day) and keep 100% of every fare they earn. There is no commission, no per-trip deduction, and no hidden fees.

> *"For too long, African drivers have lost 20-25% of every trip to platform commissions,"* said the CheeTaxi team. *"We're changing that. Drivers are the backbone of mobility — they should keep what they earn. And passengers shouldn't pay platform fees on top of the trip fare. CheeTaxi is built for Africa, by Africans, with a model that works for everyone."*

### Key features

- **12 transport modes**: taxi, ride sharing, motorcycle, three wheeler, courier, food delivery, parcel, medical, truck, scheduled, airport, corporate, school, rental, intercity, emergency
- **9 languages**: English, Amharic (አማርኛ), Oromo (Afaan Oromoo), Tigrinya, Somali, Arabic, French, Swahili, Portuguese
- **All African currencies supported**
- **24/7 safety operations center** — one-tap SOS, live trip sharing, verified drivers, response within 60 seconds
- **Modular payments**: cash, in-app wallet, card via Stripe / Chapa / Telebirr, corporate accounts
- **Driver subscriptions**: daily (Br 100), weekly (Br 500, save 28%), monthly (Br 1,800, save 40%), quarterly (Br 5,000, save 44%), yearly (Br 18,000, save 50%), plus corporate, enterprise, and government fleet plans

### Built for scale

CheeTaxi's architecture supports all 54 African countries from day one. After Addis Ababa, the platform will expand to Nairobi, Lagos, Accra, and Cape Town in 2026, with plans for pan-African coverage by 2028.

### Availability

The CheeTaxi passenger and driver apps are available for Android and iOS. Download from cheetaxi.africa or search "CheeTaxi" on Google Play or the App Store.
